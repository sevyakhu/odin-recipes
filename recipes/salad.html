Avocado Chicken Salad

Description

Creamy! Savory! Delicious!

This salad is perfect addition to a dinner party or when you want a healthy meal.

All the creamy flavors come together to form a perfect mix

Gather your ingredients ASAP!

Ingredients

Salad Ingredients
  • Shredded chicken breasts: 2
  • Avocados: 2
  • Corn: 1 cup
  • Dill
Lemon Dressing
  • Lemon juice: 3 tbsp
  • Olive oil: 3 tbsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Dice or shred the 2 large cooked chicken breasts and

    place into a large mixing bowl.

  2. Peel and pit 2 large avocados, slice into bite-sized

    pieces and add to the mixing bowl.

  3. Add 1 cup of cooked corn (freshly cooked corn is best),

    toss in 1/4 cup chopped green onion,and 2 Tbsp fresh Dill.
